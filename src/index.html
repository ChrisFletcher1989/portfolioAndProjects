REACT/EXPRESSJS/SQL:
Celebrity photo recognition AI

This smart brain is capable of recognising celebrity faces from a photo upload. After entering a photo link, it will return who the celebrity is, as well as how confident the AI is that it knows them. Made from a combination of reactJS, nodeJS, expressJS, and postgreSQL, this app features a full registration and login process before accessing the AI which is able to recognise facial features from an API.

REACT:
Tournament bracket creator for darts

This web app takes an input of how many players there will be, their names, and returns a randomly made tournament bracket determining who plays against who at each round, using a custom-built algorithm. Users can also declare seeded players, and advance players through to subsequent rounds on the UI. Made with react JS, and of course HTML + CSS. The design was centered around the layout and colors of a darts board.
